Personal loans

Applying through Axo or LendMe will save you time, money and effort

Whether you’re upgrading your car, consolidating existing debts or making home-improvements, a personal loan through Axo Finance is often more affordable than you think. You can lend from 10.000 to 500.000 DKK in Denmark, from 10.000 to 600.000 NOK/SEK in Norway and Sweden and from 2.000 € to 60.000 € in Finland. Personal loans do not require collateral.

With Axo, you apply with several banks with just one application. This is important and has high value to the customer, because interest rates on personal loans are set individually and based on your applicaiton data. Thus, you don't have to apply with every single bank to compare offers. Our online application takes just minutes to complete and as soon as all our bank partners have reviewed your application, we will contact you and present the best offer.
